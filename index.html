About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for digital, cyber and technology risk roles.

IDR bridges the gap between academic research and industry practice through collaboration with universities and real-world digital risk projects.

Service Pillars

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps designed for students and professionals in digital risk and cybersecurity.

Innovation

Research and development of future digital risk models, AI governance systems and technology frameworks.

Advisory

Helping organisations implement cybersecurity and risk frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001.

Who We Serve

Our community includes students, early-career professionals and industry practitioners working in sectors such as financial services, technology and critical infrastructure.
