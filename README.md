# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – UI + Web Development Assignment

This project is a responsive single-page website created for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)** as part of a UI + Web Development assignment.

The goal of the project is to design a simple brand identity and homepage that represents a modern digital risk training institute.

---

## Project Features

- Responsive single-page website
- Clean and minimal UI design
- Semantic HTML5 structure
- Sticky navigation bar
- Smooth scrolling navigation
- Service cards layout
- Contact form section
- Mobile-friendly layout

---

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Flexbox)
- JavaScript
- Google Fonts

No CSS frameworks were used (Bootstrap not used) as per assignment requirements.

---

## Website Sections

### 1. Hero Section
Displays the main headline representing the mission of IDR.

**Headline:**  
Advancing the Future of Digital Risk

Includes call-to-action buttons:
- Explore Programs
- Hire Talent

---

### 2. About IDR
Explains the purpose of the Institute of Digital Risk and its role in developing digital risk professionals.

---

### 3. Service Pillars

Three core service areas are presented using cards:

- **Academy**  
Training and bootcamps for students and professionals in digital risk.

- **Innovation**  
Research and development of future risk models and AI governance systems.

- **Advisory**  
Helping organizations implement cybersecurity frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001.

---

### 4. Community
Describes the community served by IDR including students, early-career professionals, and industry practitioners.

---

### 5. Contact Section
A simple contact form allowing users to submit their interest.

Fields include:
- Name
- Email
- Message

---

## Logo Design Concept

The IDR logo uses a **cube-inspired geometric design** to represent structure, resilience, and security in digital risk environments.

The color palette includes:

- **Orange** – innovation and technology leadership  
- **Black** – professionalism and strength  
- **White** – simplicity and clarity  

The minimalist design ensures readability even at small sizes such as favicons or mobile headers.
